AI Training Data Transparency Disclosure

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Compliance: California Generative AI Training Data Transparency Act (Effective January 1, 2026)

1. AI System Information

PipeWrench AI utilizes Anthropic's Claude AI models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) as the underlying generative AI system. This disclosure pertains to the training data used by Anthropic to develop these models.

2. Data Sources and Ownership

The Claude AI models are trained by Anthropic PBC. According to Anthropic's public disclosures:

  • Training data includes publicly available information from the internet, licensed datasets, and data provided by users who have consented to its use for training purposes.
  • Data sources support the system's intended purpose of natural language understanding, generation, and reasoning.
  • Anthropic maintains detailed documentation of data provenance and licensing.

3. Dataset Size and Characteristics

  • Size: Claude models are trained on datasets containing hundreds of billions of tokens of text data.
  • Data Types: Text-based data including books, articles, websites, code repositories, and other written content.
  • Labeling: Data includes both unlabeled text and human-labeled examples for specific tasks.

4. Intellectual Property Considerations

  • Training datasets may include copyrighted material used under fair use principles and licensed content.
  • Anthropic implements filtering to reduce inclusion of copyrighted material where appropriate.
  • Public domain content is included in training data.
  • Anthropic respects intellectual property rights and provides mechanisms for rights holders to request content removal.

5. Data Acquisition

  • Some datasets are licensed from third-party providers.
  • Publicly available data is collected through web scraping and other automated methods.
  • User-contributed data is obtained with appropriate consent.

6. Personal Information

  • Training data may contain personal information that was publicly available on the internet.
  • Anthropic implements privacy-preserving techniques to minimize retention of personal information.
  • The system does not intentionally memorize or reproduce personal information from training data.
  • No aggregate consumer information as defined under California law is knowingly included for the purpose of profiling.

7. Data Processing and Modifications

  • Cleaning: Data undergoes filtering to remove low-quality, toxic, or harmful content.
  • Deduplication: Duplicate content is identified and removed to improve training efficiency.
  • Formatting: Data is processed into formats suitable for model training.
  • Purpose: These modifications improve model quality, safety, and performance.

8. Data Collection Periods

  • Training data was collected over multiple years prior to model training.
  • Claude 3.5 Sonnet (released October 2024) has a knowledge cutoff of April 2024.
  • Data collection is ongoing for future model versions.
  • First used in development: Training data collection began in 2021 for early Claude models.

9. Synthetic Data

  • Synthetic data generation techniques are used to augment training data.
  • Functional Need: Synthetic data helps improve model performance on specific tasks, enhance safety, and fill gaps in naturally occurring data.
  • Synthetic data is generated using earlier AI models and human oversight.

10. Exemptions

This system is not exempt under California law. It is not exclusively provided to federal entities for national security purposes, nor is it exclusively provided to hospital medical staff members.

11. Updates and Modifications

This disclosure will be updated when substantial modifications are made to the AI system, including retraining or fine-tuning that materially affects performance. Last substantial modification: October 2024 (Claude 3.5 Sonnet release).

12. Additional Information

For detailed information about Anthropic's training data and practices, please visit:

  • Anthropic's Official Website
  • Anthropic's Privacy Policy

13. Contact for Data Transparency Inquiries

For questions regarding AI training data transparency, contact: transparency@pipewrenchai.com
